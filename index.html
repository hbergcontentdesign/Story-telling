Facility Manager's Perspective

The Facility Manager oversees the entire operation. From here, you can manage overall facility efficiency, assign work orders, monitor KPIs, and ensure compliance. Use dashboards to track asset health, schedule preventive maintenance, and allocate resources effectively.

Technician's Perspective

The Technician focuses on work orders and assigned tasks. You can view detailed maintenance instructions, update the status of ongoing repairs, and mark tasks as completed. Everything needed is at your fingertips, ensuring efficiency and prioritization of critical jobs.

Vendor's Perspective

The Vendor provides essential services to the facility. From this view, you can receive work orders, submit quotes, and track job progress. Manage your interactions with the facility efficiently to ensure smooth operations and transparency.
